# New Patient Guide

Welcome to MacLeod Naturopathic! This guide will help you prepare for your first visit and understand what to expect from your naturopathic care.

## Before Your First Visit

### What to Bring
- **Photo ID** and insurance cards
- **List of current medications** (including supplements and herbs)
- **Recent lab results** (within 6 months if available)
- **Medical records** from other healthcare providers
- **Completed intake forms** (sent via email prior to appointment)

### Preparation Tips
- **Get adequate sleep** the night before your appointment
- **Eat normally** unless specifically instructed otherwise
- **Arrive 10 minutes early** for check-in
- **Prepare questions** you'd like to discuss with Dr. MacLeod

## Your Initial Visit (60 minutes)

### Comprehensive Health Assessment
Dr. MacLeod will conduct a thorough review including:
- **Detailed health history** - current concerns, past medical history, family history
- **Lifestyle assessment** - diet, exercise, sleep patterns, stress levels
- **Physical examination** as appropriate
- **Review of systems** - comprehensive evaluation of all body systems

### Discussion and Treatment Planning
- **Root cause analysis** of your health concerns
- **Personalized treatment plan** recommendations
- **Lab testing** recommendations if needed
- **Lifestyle modifications** and dietary guidance
- **Treatment goals** and timeline expectations

## Follow-Up Care

### Regular Monitoring
- **Follow-up visits** typically scheduled every 2-4 weeks initially
- **Progress tracking** and treatment plan adjustments
- **Ongoing support** for lifestyle changes
- **Lab test interpretation** and health monitoring

### Communication Between Visits
- **Email support** for urgent questions
- **Appointment reminders** via phone or email
- **Lab results** communicated promptly with explanation

# IV Therapy Patient Information

Intravenous (IV) therapy delivers nutrients directly into your bloodstream, achieving therapeutic blood levels that cannot be reached through oral supplementation alone.

## What is IV Therapy?

IV therapy involves the administration of vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients directly into the bloodstream through a small catheter placed in your arm. This method bypasses the digestive system, allowing for:

- **100% absorption** of nutrients
- **Rapid therapeutic effects** 
- **Higher blood concentrations** than oral supplementation
- **Immediate availability** to cells and tissues

## Preparing for Your IV Treatment

### 24 Hours Before
- **Stay well hydrated** - drink plenty of water
- **Eat regular meals** - don't come on an empty stomach
- **Get adequate sleep** for optimal response
- **Continue regular medications** unless instructed otherwise

### Day of Treatment
- **Eat a light meal** 1-2 hours before treatment
- **Bring water** to drink during treatment
- **Wear comfortable clothing** with easy access to your arms
- **Bring entertainment** - book, tablet, or music (treatment takes 30-90 minutes)

## What to Expect During Treatment

### The Process
1. **Consultation** - Brief review of health and treatment goals
2. **Vital signs** - Blood pressure and pulse monitoring
3. **IV insertion** - Small catheter placed in arm vein
4. **Treatment administration** - Nutrients infused slowly over 30-90 minutes
5. **Monitoring** - Regular check-ins during treatment
6. **Completion** - IV removed, brief post-treatment assessment

### During the IV
- **Minimal discomfort** from initial needle insertion
- **Relaxing environment** - comfortable reclining chairs
- **Slight coolness** - normal sensation as fluids enter bloodstream
- **Metallic taste** - temporary taste with some nutrients (especially B vitamins)

## After Your IV Treatment

### Immediate Post-Treatment
- **Drink plenty of water** - continue hydration
- **Eat a light snack** if hungry
- **Apply pressure** to injection site if any bleeding
- **Normal activities** can be resumed immediately

### What You Might Experience
**Positive Effects (often immediate):**
- Increased energy and alertness
- Improved mood and mental clarity
- Enhanced sense of well-being
- Better sleep quality

**Temporary Side Effects (rare):**
- Slight soreness at injection site
- Temporary fatigue (as body processes nutrients)
- Increased urination (normal detox response)

### When to Contact Us
- **Persistent pain** or swelling at injection site
- **Signs of infection** (redness, warmth, fever)
- **Unusual symptoms** or concerns
- **Questions** about your response to treatment

## Treatment Recommendations

### Frequency
- **Initial series:** 4-6 treatments over 2-3 weeks
- **Maintenance:** Monthly or as recommended
- **Acute conditions:** May require more frequent treatments
- **Chronic conditions:** Regular ongoing treatments

### Optimization
- **Lab testing** to identify specific nutrient needs
- **Customized formulas** based on individual requirements
- **Progress monitoring** and treatment adjustments
- **Lifestyle integration** with diet and supplement recommendations

## Safety and Contraindications

### IV Therapy is Generally Safe When:
- Administered by trained healthcare professionals
- Proper screening is performed
- Quality nutrients and sterile technique used
- Appropriate monitoring is maintained

### Contraindications
- **Kidney dysfunction** (certain formulas)
- **Heart failure** (fluid restrictions)
- **Active infection** at potential IV site
- **Pregnancy** (specific nutrients only)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does each treatment take?**  
A: Typically 30-90 minutes depending on the specific formula and your tolerance.

**Q: How will I feel during the treatment?**  
A: Most patients find it very relaxing. You may feel a slight coolness as the IV flows.

**Q: When will I notice benefits?**  
A: Many patients feel increased energy and well-being within hours to days after treatment.

**Q: Are there any side effects?**  
A: Side effects are rare but may include temporary soreness at the injection site.

**Q: How many treatments will I need?**  
A: This varies by individual and condition. Most people benefit from an initial series of 4-6 treatments.
